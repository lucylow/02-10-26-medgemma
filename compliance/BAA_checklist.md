# Business Associate Agreement (BAA) Checklist

**For HIPAA-covered entities. Use with cloud vendors (GCP, AWS, etc.).**

---

## Items to Include

- [ ] Permitted uses and disclosures of PHI
- [ ] Safeguards (encryption, access controls)
- [ ] Breach notification obligations and timeline
- [ ] Subprocessor rules and prior consent
- [ ] Return/destruction of PHI on termination
- [ ] Audit rights
- [ ] Indemnification
